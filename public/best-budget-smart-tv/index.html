What is the Best Budget Smart TV?

January 13, 2025 · 1 min · joshuapsteele | Suggest Changes

In November 2024, I bought a 55-inch Hisense U6 4K Smart TV on sale for $350. It’s been great so far! Definitely an upgrade over our old Samsung.

I’m not a TV expert, but I did a fair bit of research and the consensus seems to be that the Hisense U6 series is a great deal for the money.
