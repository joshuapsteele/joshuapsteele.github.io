Caveats

Caveats Please keep these points in mind when reading what I write. Provoking Thought, Not Debate My intent is (almost always) to provoke thought, not start a debate or argument. “I’m not Y!” You’re probably right! If my words don’t apply to you, please feel free to ignore them and move along. “Not all X! You left out Z!” I recognize that reality is complex and nuanced. Communication is always an oversimplification of reality. I try to value brevity, so I often keep things punchy and to the point when sharing online. ...
