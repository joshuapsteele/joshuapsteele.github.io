## My Cardio Workout

First, I have to call out the ["John Terry Treadmill Workout"](https://www.thickaccent.com/2023/07/28/the-john-terry-treadmill-workout-leaving-fans-begging-for-mercy/), because it's what's been most helpful in (slowly) getting me back into soccer shape. It's brutal, but effective. As [this Reddit post clarifies](https://www.reddit.com/r/bootroom/comments/bbh3h6/get_match_fit_quick_the_john_terry_routine/): 

- Warm up for around 5 mins or so
- Set your treadmill to a 12% incline
- Turn up the speed. Terry does his at 18 km/h (11.2 mph) but if you're not used to running at that speed, start lower and work your way up. I'm currently at 8.2 mph!
- Run for 20 seconds, then rest for 40 seconds. I usually leave the treadmill running at the full speed and just step off, either fully or with a leg on each side.
- Repeat for 15 rounds
