What I Want

By joshuapsteele

January 17, 2023 - 2 minutes read - 275 words

I want…

  • To be a good husband
  • To be a good father
  • To be a good friend
  • To solve people’s problems
  • To build what people use
  • To write what people read
  • To never stop learning

Finances

  • To have control over my time
    • “The ability to do what you want, when you want, with who you want, for as long as you want, is priceless. It is the highest dividend money pays” (Morgan Housel, The Psychology of Money, 83).
  • To make at least $200K/year
  • To have at least $100K saved in the bank

Home

  • To own a house with
    • central AC,
    • dry basement,
    • home office,
    • workshop, and
    • home gym

Health & Fitness

  • To weigh 200 pounds or less
  • To be able to run a half marathon at any time
  • To look good naked
  • Power rack with a pull-up bar (like the REP Fitness PR–1100 or Rogue R–3)
  • Deadlift platform (build my own with horse stall mats from Tractor Supply Co.)

Books

  • Algorithms (Sedgewick and Wayne)
  • The Clean Coder (Martin)
  • Clean Architecture (Martin)
  • Clean Craftsmanship (Martin)
  • Code That Fits in Your Head (Seemann)
  • Introduction to Algorithms (Cormen et al.)

Tools

  • Workbench (like this workbench from Harbor Freight)
  • Table saw
  • Miter saw
  • Reciprocating saw, AKA “Sawzall”)
  • Pocket hole jig
  • Hand truck
  • Floor jack
  • Jack stands
  • Stanley FuBar III pry bar
  • “Burke bar” pry bar

Tech

  • Supernote A5X tablet, pen, and folio cover
  • XBox (Series X or S) console, controllers, and headsets

Miscellaneous

  • Electric guitar, amp, and pedals (something like this Yamaha GigMaker set)
  • Rifle (something like a Ruger 10/22)
  • Shotgun (something like a Remington 870 or Mossberg 500)
  • Handgun (perhaps a Glock G19 Gen 5)
